# Sales and Profit Excel Dashboard
<br>
This project shows an interactive **Sales and Profit Dashboard** built in Microsoft Excel.  
It provides a comprehensive view of business performance through clear visualizations and key metrics.

## ✅ Key Features

- **[Key Metrics Overview](ca://s?q=Key_Metrics_Overview_in_Excel_Dashboard)**  
  Quick snapshot of **total sales** and **total profit** for instant performance insights.

- **[Profit by Year Chart](ca://s?q=Profit_by_Year_Chart_in_Excel)**  
  Visualizes yearly profit trends to identify growth opportunities.

- **[Sales Breakdown by Category](ca://s?q=Sales_Breakdown_by_Category_in_Excel)**  
  Detailed distribution of sales across product categories.

- **[Customer Count by Year](ca://s?q=Customer_Count_by_Year_in_Excel)**  
  Pie chart analysis of customer engagement trends over time.

- **[Sales by State Map](ca://s?q=Sales_by_State_Map_in_Excel)**  
  Geographical heatmap to understand regional performance.

- **[Monthly Sales Trends](ca://s?q=Monthly_Sales_Trends_in_Excel)**  
  Easy-to-read monthly trend lines to spot seasonal patterns.

- **[Top 5 Customers Profits](ca://s?q=Top_5_Customers_Profits_in_Excel)**  
  Highlights the most valuable customers driving profitability.
